Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Who can donate blood?

Most healthy people over 17 who weigh at least 110 pounds can donate blood. Some medical conditions may prevent donation.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days. Platelet and plasma donation intervals may vary.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is safe. All equipment is sterile and used only once.
